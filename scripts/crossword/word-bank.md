# End-of-Year Crossword — Word Bank

Source vocabulary and clues for the Day 43 (Wed 5/20/2026) end-of-year crossword puzzle.

The crossword generator at `scripts/crossword/generate.py` reads each `## Period N — Words and Clues` table below: column 1 is the answer (uppercase, no spaces) and column 2 is the clue printed in the puzzle. Each period gets its own PDF (`end-of-year-crossword.pdf` for period 1, `end-of-year-crossword-p2.pdf` for period 2, and so on) so students from one period cannot share answers with another. Clues are written so an 8th-grader who paid attention this year can solve them without resources.

The lists are intentionally tighter than the word-search bank — crosswords need words that interlock cleanly. Multi-word terms (`TRAININGDATA`), very short words (`IF`), and proper nouns are omitted.
