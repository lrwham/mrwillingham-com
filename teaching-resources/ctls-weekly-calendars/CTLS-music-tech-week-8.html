Music Technology — Week 8

Unit: Film Scoring

This week is dedicated to finishing the film scoring group project. The schedule is unusual because of the 6th, 7th, and 8th grade field days. Monday begins with a BrainPOP on Filmmaking, then the rest of the period — and the rest of the week — is a quiet, focused work session on each student's individual part of the film. Each group member works on only their own part (Part 1, Part 2, Part 3, etc.); Mr. Willingham combines the parts into the finished movie after submission. Tuesday and Wednesday are project workdays for whichever classes meet around the field days. No classes Thursday. Final parts are due Friday.
