Music Technology — Week 3

Units: Podcasts & Sound Design

This week you will finish and share your podcast episode on Monday, then begin a new unit on Sound Design. You'll explore sound design using a Rocket League animation, produce effects for an Agent 327 film clip, record found sounds from the environment, and collaborate as a class to design a Minecraft character sound effect.

Day Date Topic Summary
11 Mon 3/30 Finish Podcast Finish your podcast, export it as an MP3, upload it to CTLS, and listen to and comment on other students' shows.
12 Tue 3/31 Rocket League Sound Design Import a Rocket League animation into GarageBand, place and align provided audio files to key moments in the video, and use effects like reverb and pitch shift to shape the sounds.
13 Wed 4/1 Agent 327 - Operation Barbershop Use at least five prepared sound effects in GarageBand to produce the sound design for a 30-second clip of the Agent 327 film, adding plugins, automation, and library loops for music.
14 Thu 4/2 Sound Design From Scratch Record found sounds from around the classroom using portable recorders, then use GarageBand plugins like reverb, delay, distortion, and pitch shift to transform them into original sound effects.
15 Fri 4/3 Minecraft Sound Design Review sound design concepts as a class, then collaborate to design an original GarageBand sound effect for a class-voted Minecraft character using layered found sounds and plugin effects.
