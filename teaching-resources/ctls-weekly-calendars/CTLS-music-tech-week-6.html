Music Technology — Week 6

Unit: Musical Form and Melody Writing

This week we learn to write melodies using common musical forms. You'll explore ABA form, binary form, 32-bar form, and how chord progressions support a melody. By Friday you'll compose and share a final melody that brings together everything from the week.
