Programming with Scratch — Week 5

Unit: Video Game Design Project

This week you begin your final project — designing and building a video game in Scratch with a team. Over these two weeks you will brainstorm your game concept, create concept art, write a Game Design Document, build a working prototype in Scratch, and present your finished game to the class.
