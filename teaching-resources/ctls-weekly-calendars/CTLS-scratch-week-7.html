Programming with Scratch — Week 7

Unit: Python and the Terminal

This week you move from Scratch to Python. Monday and Tuesday cover the basics — installing Python, using VS Code, and writing your first interactive program. Wednesday and Thursday we use Python to explore real-world data with the pandas and matplotlib libraries, starting with a Pokémon dataset. On Friday we shift to a conceptual introduction to artificial intelligence and machine learning through BrainPOP.
