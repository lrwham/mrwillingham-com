Music Technology — Week 2

Unit: Podcasts

This week you will write, record, and edit your own podcast episode. You'll create a script, compose intro music in GarageBand, give and receive peer feedback, then record and edit your final episode in Soundtrap.

Day Date Topic Summary
6 Mon 3/23 Write Your Script Review script expectations and write a full podcast script — introduction, main content, and conclusion — with audio annotations in Word Online.
7 Tue 3/24 Intro Music Use GarageBand to layer 2–4 loops and create a 10–20 second original intro music track, then export it as an MP3 to OneDrive.
8 Wed 3/25 Peer Feedback & Final Script Share your script with two classmates for peer review, then revise and finalize it so it is ready to record.
9 Thu 3/26 Record Your Podcast Set up the microphone and audio interface, import your intro music, and record your full podcast episode one section at a time in Soundtrap.
10 Fri 3/27 Edit & Share Trim mistakes, balance levels, add sound effects and outro music in Soundtrap, then export the finished episode as an MP3 and upload it to CTLS.
Graded Assignments
  • Podcast Script (due Wednesday 3/25) — Your finalized script must meet all expectations and be ready for recording by end of day.
  • Finished Podcast (due Friday 3/27) — Your recorded and edited podcast episode, exported as an MP3 and shared on CTLS.

Late work will receive a one-time 20 point deduction.
