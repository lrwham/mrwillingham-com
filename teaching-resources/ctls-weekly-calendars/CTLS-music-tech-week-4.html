Music Technology — Week 4

Unit: Beat Making

This week you will begin a new unit on Beat Making. You'll learn the parts of a drum kit, explore drum loops, and use a MIDI controller to write your own drum beats in GarageBand.

Day Date Topic Summary
16 Mon 4/13 Click here => Introduction to Drums Learn the parts of a drum kit and build a beat from individual loops.
17 Tue 4/14 Click here => MIDI & the Piano Roll Learn what MIDI is and how the piano roll works. Use MIDI drum mappings to program your first beat in GarageBand.
18 Wed 4/15 Writing a Drum Beat Use the MIDI controller to write and record a drum beat in GarageBand.
19 Thu 4/16 Fills & Beat Forms Introduce drum fills and basic beat forms to add structure to your beats.
20 Fri 4/17 Finish Your Beat Put it all together — finish a full drum beat with grooves, fills, and form.
