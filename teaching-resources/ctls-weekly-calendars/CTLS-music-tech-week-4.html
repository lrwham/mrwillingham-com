Music Technology — Week 4

Unit: Beat Making

This week you will begin a new unit on Beat Making. You'll learn the parts of a drum kit, explore drum loops, and use a MIDI controller and Musical Typing to record and quantize your own four-on-the-floor drum beat in GarageBand.

Day Date Topic Summary
16 Mon 4/13 Introduction to Drums Learn the parts of a drum kit and build a beat from individual loops in GarageBand.

20 Fri 4/17 Finish Your Beat Add the hi-hat and a choice sound to complete the four-on-the-floor beat started on Thursday, then quantize all four tracks.
