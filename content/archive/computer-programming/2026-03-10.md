`Events` are a fundamental concept in programming. They allow us to trigger actions in our program when something happens. For example,

1. When the green flag is clicked, we can start our program.
1. When a sprite is clicked, we can make it say something or change its costume.
1. When a mouse is moved, we can make a sprite follow it.

For video game programming, `events` are essential.
