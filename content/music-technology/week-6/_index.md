## Unit: Musical Form and Melody Writing

This week students explore musical form by composing original melodies in MuseScore and GarageBand. Days 26–28 build progressively in MuseScore: you compose an ABA-form melody in C major, add a bass line to a binary-form piece, and then write chord progressions with melodies in both C major and A minor. Days 29–30 shift to GarageBand, where you import MIDI chord progressions, arrange them into AABA (32-bar) form with drums and an arpeggiator, and compose an original melody on top — finishing with an MP3 export submitted to CTLS.

## Weekly Schedule

| Day | Date     | Topic                                                              | Summary                                                                                                                             |
| --- | -------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 26  | Mon 4/27 | [ABA Form and the Tonic Note](day-26/)                             | Students compose a 12-measure original melody in C major using ABA form in MuseScore, then share it on a CTLS discussion post for peer feedback. |
| 27  | Tue 4/28 | [Binary Form and Bass Lines](day-27/)                              | Students choose one binary-form melody, mark its A and B sections in MuseScore, and add a one-note-per-measure bass line in C major or A minor. |
| 28  | Wed 4/29 | [Chord Progressions and Melody](day-28/)                           | Students enter I–V–vi–IV chords for an 8-measure A section in C major and i–VII–VI–VII chords for an 8-measure B section in A minor, then write a melody over each in MuseScore. |
| 29  | Thu 4/30 | [32-Bar Form and Chords in GarageBand](day-29/)                    | Students import a MIDI chord progression into GarageBand, duplicate it into AABA (32-bar) form, add a drum loop, try the arpeggiator, and begin recording a melody. |
| 30  | Fri 5/1  | [Finish Your 32-Bar Melody](day-30/)                               | Students complete their AABA project with chords, drums, and an original melody, then export it as an MP3 and submit to the Day 30 CTLS discussion post. |

- **Day 30** — Export your completed 32-bar AABA project as an MP3 and submit it to the Day 30 discussion post on CTLS.
