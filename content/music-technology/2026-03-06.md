{{< warmup "Review the Project" >}}
In this project you are producing a complete podcast. So far we have been focused on writing the script. The script should contain the exact words you intend to say when we record the episode Monday.

Last week, while Mr. Willingham was away riding rollercoasters, you should have prepared some music in Soundtrap. You will use that music in your episode. We'll add the music **after** we record.

Similarly, we will add sound effects after we record. Soundtrap has a sound library with a variety of sound effects. You should include the name of these sound effects on your script where you intend to use them. For example, you might write [Insert Applause Sound Effect Here] in your script to indicate where you want to add an applause sound effect.

After you finish writing today, you will go to Soundtrap and find these sound effects. Update your script to reflect the names of the sound effects you intend to use. This will help you remember to add them when we edit the episode.

Here are the [script expectations](../podcast-script-expectations/).

Login to [Office 365](https://www.office.com/) and continue your work. Make revisions to your script so far based on the feedback you received yesterday. Then, finish the rest of the show.
