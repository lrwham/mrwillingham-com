## Work Session: Sound Check

We will set up the microphone and audio interface together, then each record a short sound check in GarageBand. This is practice for the podcast recording sessions coming up soon.

### Setup Checklist

Follow these steps in order:

1. Connect the microphone to the audio interface using the **XLR cable**.
1. Connect the audio interface to the computer using the **USB cable**.
1. Turn the **gain knob** up to an appropriate level (not too quiet, not too loud).
1. Position the microphone about **6 inches from your mouth**.
1. Open **GarageBand** and create a new empty project.
1. Add a new **audio track** with the input set to your audio interface.

### Record a Sound Check

Once your setup is complete:

1. Press the **record button** in GarageBand.
1. Speak clearly into the microphone at your normal volume. Say your name, today's date, and count to ten.
1. Stop the recording and **listen back**.
1. If the recording is too quiet, turn the gain up slightly and try again. If it's too loud or distorted, turn the gain down.
1. Adjust until your voice sounds clear and the levels stay in the green/yellow range (not red).
