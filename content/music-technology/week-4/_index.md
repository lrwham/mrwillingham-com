## Unit: Beat Making

This week we start making beats! You'll learn the parts of a drum kit, use loops to build beats, and get hands-on with a MIDI controller to write your own drum patterns.

## Weekly Schedule
| Day | Date     | Topic                                                    | Summary                                                                              |
| --- | -------- | -------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| 16  | Mon 4/13 | [Introduction to Drums](day-16/)                         | Learn the parts of a drum kit and build a beat from individual loops.                |
| 17  | Tue 4/14 | [MIDI & the Piano Roll](day-17/)                         | Learn what MIDI is and how the piano roll works. Use MIDI drum mappings to program your first beat in GarageBand. |
| 18  | Wed 4/15 | [Writing a Drum Beat](day-18/)                           | Transcribe the "Rock Steady" beat from the printed packet into GarageBand's piano roll. |
| 19  | Thu 4/16 | Fills & Beat Forms                                       | Introduce drum fills and basic beat forms to add structure to your beats.             |
| 20  | Fri 4/17 | Finish Your Beat                                         | Put it all together — finish a full drum beat with grooves, fills, and form.         |
