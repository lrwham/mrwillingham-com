## Unit: Remixing

This week you will remix a piece of classical music into a new style, then shift into a group project where you compose and record original music for a short animated film.

## Weekly Schedule

| Day | Date    | Topic                                              | Summary                                                                                                       |
| --- | ------- | -------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 31  | Mon 5/4 | [Remix — Choose Your Track](day-31/)               | Download two classical MIDI files, import them into GarageBand, listen to both, and pick one to remix.        |
| 32  | Tue 5/5 | [Remix — Work Session](day-32/)                    | Continue building your remix — add loops, change instruments, adjust tempo, and use automation.               |
| 33  | Wed 5/6 | [Remix — Finish and Share](day-33/)                | Finish your remix, export it as an MP3, and share it with the class. **Remix due today.**                     |
| 34  | Thu 5/7 | [Group Project — Day 1](day-34/)                   | Form groups of 2–3, get assigned a short animated film, and begin composing music to match the action.        |
| 35  | Fri 5/8 | [Group Project — Day 2](day-35/)                   | Continue scoring your film — sync music and sound effects to key moments in the video.                        |
