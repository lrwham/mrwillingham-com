In Fruit Mash, the objective of the game is to collect as many fruits as possible before the timer runs out. There are certain types of fruits that you can obtain, but also bad types that will lower your score. You will be given two minutes to collect strawberries, blueberries, kiwi, pineapple, bananas, and blackberries. The ones that will be rotten are watermelon, apples, and oranges. The more fruits that are not rotten, the higher the score you will get. Fruit Mash will be found in the app store today!
