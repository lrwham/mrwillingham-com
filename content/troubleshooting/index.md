## Sound Issues

If you have issues with the sound on your computer...

1. Check that the blue Audio Interface is plugged in.
1. Open the volume settings page with `option` + `🔊` (option volume)
    - In the volume settings page, make sure "Presonus AudioBox iTwo" is selected as the output device.
    - On your keyboard, the option key may be labeled `alt` or `opt` or `⌥`
    - ![Select the AudioBox iTwo](select-audio-interface.png)
1. Check that the `Mix` knob on the AudioBox iTwo is turned all the way to the right (towards `Playback`).
    - ![Mix Knob](mix-knob.jpg)
1. Adjust the `Volume` knob on the AudioBox iTwo. This is the **small** knob next to where the headphones are plugged in.
    - ![Volume Knob](volume-knob.jpg)

## Finding Apps

Use the `Command Space` shortcut to open Spotlight Search. Then type in the name of the app you want. Double click on the app to open it.

---

## Logging Off

Type in `Command Shift Q` to log off of your computer.

If someone forgot to log off and the computer is now locked, you will need to turn of the computer by `holding the power button` for **ten seconds**.

---

## Copy & Paste

On the Macs, copy and paste is a bit different. Use the `command` key instead of the `control` key.

Copy is `Command C`

Paste is `Command V`

---

## Closing Apps

To close an app completely (especially because an app is misbehaving), try `Command Q` to quit an app.

Force quit is a special option if needed. To force quit an app, press `Command Option Escape` and then select the app you want to force quit.
