Music Technology — Week 7

Unit: Remixing and Film Scoring

This week you will remix a piece of classical music into a new style using GarageBand, then work in a small group to compose and record original music for a short animated film. Monday through Wednesday are dedicated to the remix — you'll choose a classical MIDI file from the Mutopia Project, rearrange it, change instruments, adjust the tempo, add loops, and use automation. Thursday and Friday you'll form groups of 2–3, get assigned a film, and begin scoring it from scratch.
