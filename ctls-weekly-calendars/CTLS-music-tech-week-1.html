Music Technology — Week 1

Unit: Podcasts

In this unit, you will create your own podcast episode from start to finish. You'll learn the science of sound, how to set up and use recording equipment, and how to write, record, and edit a podcast. At the end of the unit, you'll share your finished episode with the class.
